Transit-Oriented Opportunity in Washington, DC

This is a map of Rock Creek West, one of ten planning areas in Washington, DC. The Washington metro's Red Line runs through Rock Creek West. A 15-minute walking radius from each station in Rock Creek West is shown.

Click on a metro station button below, or the walkshed on the map directly, to zoom toward and inspect what is accsesible a 15-minute walk of the station.
