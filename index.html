Transit-Oriented Opportunity in Rock Creek West

This is a map of Rock Creek West, the wealthiest of Washington, DC's ten planning areas. Five stations on the Washington metro's Red Line are within Rock Creek West.

You can see the 15-minute walking distance from each station, and the intended future land use for each area, according to the District's Comprehensive Plan.
